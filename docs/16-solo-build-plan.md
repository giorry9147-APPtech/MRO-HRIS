# Solo Build Plan (12 Weeks)

## Strategy

Build by business flow, not by random screens:

1. Foundation
2. Organization
3. Functions and positions
4. Employees
5. Documents
6. Salary
7. Assets
8. Reports
9. Hardening and launch

## Weekly Plan

### Week 1
- Backend and frontend setup
- PostgreSQL connection
- Auth baseline
- Roles and permissions baseline

### Week 2
- Directorates CRUD
- Departments CRUD

### Week 3
- Pay scales CRUD
- Job functions CRUD

### Week 4
- Positions CRUD
- Position statuses and hierarchy

### Week 5
- Employees CRUD
- Filters and search

### Week 6
- Employment records and history
- Transfer and exit baseline

### Week 7
- Document types
- Secure upload/download

### Week 8
- Qualifications and work experience

### Week 9
- Salary assignments
- Overlap validations

### Week 10
- Assets CRUD
- Assignment and return flows

### Week 11
- Reports and exports
- Audit logging on critical actions

### Week 12
- Bug fixing
- Permissions review
- Backup and deployment checks

## Definition Of Done (MVP)

- Login and role-based access works
- Organization, functions, positions and employees are manageable
- Employment history is recorded
- Documents are stored privately and access-controlled
- Salary assignments are stored with valid date ranges
- Assets can be assigned and returned
- Core reports are available
- Critical actions are auditable
